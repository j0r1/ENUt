ENUt

Welcome to the home page of ENUt.

Introduction

The name ENUt stands for 'EDM Network Utilities'. This library was developed at the Expertise Centre for Digital Media (EDM), a research institute of the Hasselt University.

The latest version of the library is 1.1.2 (July 2012).

License

The license that applies to the library is the LGPL.
